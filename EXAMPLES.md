## Key Patterns for Success

### 1. Lead with Reputation
Always start with their positive reviews/rating. It's a compliment and shows you did research.

### 2. Identify the Gap
Point out the specific problem (no website, weak SEO, no booking system).

### 3. Quantify the Loss
Use numbers: "losing X customers/day" or "missing $X/month".

### 4. Offer Solution
Make it about them, not you. "Want to fix that?" not "Buy my service".

### 5. Use Opportunity Signals
LeadStrike gives you the exact pain points in `opportunity_signals`. Use them!

## Conversion Math

### HOT Leads (Score 80+)
- **Response rate:** 40-60%
- **Meeting rate:** 25-40%
- **Close rate:** 15-30%
- **Example:** 100 HOT leads → 15-30 closed deals

### WARM Leads (Score 60-79)
- **Response rate:** 20-35%
- **Meeting rate:** 15-25%
- **Close rate:** 8-15%
- **Example:** 100 WARM leads → 8-15 closed deals

### COLD Leads (Score <60)
- **Response rate:** 5-15%
- **Meeting rate:** 3-8%
- **Close rate:** 1-5%
- **Example:** 100 COLD leads → 1-5 closed deals

**Focus on HOT leads for maximum ROI.**

## Pro Tips

### 1. Niche Down
Don't target "all businesses". Pick one niche, dominate it, then expand.

### 2. Geographic Focus
Start with your city. Local = easier to close (can meet in person).

### 3. Batch Your Outreach
Export 50 HOT leads, contact all in one week. Track results. Iterate.

### 4. Use Multi-Channel
Phone + Email + LinkedIn = 3x response rate vs single channel.

### 5. Follow Up
80% of deals close after 5+ touchpoints. Don't give up after one email.

### 6. Track Your Numbers
LeadStrike gives you the leads. You need to track:
- Contacted
- Responded
- Meeting booked
- Proposal sent
- Closed

This lets you optimize your pitch and process.

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Targeting COLD Leads
If score < 60, they probably don't need you urgently. Focus on HOT.

### ❌ Generic Outreach
"I can help your business" = ignored. Use their specific signals.

### ❌ Selling Too Early
First contact = start conversation. Second contact = offer value. Third = pitch.

### ❌ Ignoring Phone Numbers
Email open rate = 20%. Phone pickup rate = 60%. Call them!

### ❌ Not Following Up
Most deals happen after 3-7 touchpoints. Persistence wins.
