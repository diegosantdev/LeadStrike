## 🎯 Revenue Leak Index™

Each lead gets scored (0–100) based on the gap between reputation and visibility.

| Score | Label | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 🔥 80–100 | **HOT** | Losing money NOW, act fast |
| 🟡 60–79 | **WARM** | Revenue leak detected |
| ⚪ < 60 | **COLD** | Low urgency |

### Opportunity Signals™

| Factor | Weight | Why It Matters |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Digital weakness | 40 pts | No website = 70% traffic loss |
| Reviews volume | 30 pts | Proven demand |
| Rating quality | 25 pts | Customer trust |
| Contact available | 20 pts | Sales-ready |
| Active status | 10 pts | Money on the table |
